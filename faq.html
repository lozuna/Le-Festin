FAQs

  • What are the policies on refunds?

If the jewelry is broken when you recieve it, refunds or exchanges are offered.

  • Do you take custom orders?

Yes! Message me for inquiries.

  • What is the proper jewelry care?

Keep away from water, moisture, lotions, perfumes, etc. to avoid tarnishing. Be gentle! Do not stretch or apply excessive pressure in order to prevent jewelry from breaking.

  • How long is the processing time?

Orders will be packaged and shipped within 2-5 days.
